Item 6.

A map of the \(29\) major municipalities in Walworth County, WI, colored according to medical need from the final model. Filled circles of radius \(4500\) meters are placed at the centroids of Whitewater city, Delavan city, and Bloomfield village, representing coverage by clinics placed at these three municipalities. The centroids of Bloomfield village, Bloomfield town, Fontana-on-Geneva Lake village, Walworth village, Walworth town, Whitewater city, Whitewater town, and Williams Bay village, all lie inside the filled circles, indicating that the medical need in these municipalities is covered under this model.
